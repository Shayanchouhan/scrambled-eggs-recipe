Srambled Eggs Recipe

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich sceambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium hear.
  2. Gently mix, the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the center every 20 second (as if you are making an omelette
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
